About Me

Hi, my name is Jonathan Böttcher, I was born in 1999 in Berlin, but spent most of my adolescence in Southwest Germany between the Cities Of Freiburg and Lörrach. I came in contact with Music Production at the Age of 14 and started DJing at the age of 18. At that time I also discovered Psytrance and started to organise my own small Events. I am a trained Car Mechanic, but my Heart beats for the Music. Currently I am Studying Media Conception at Hochschule Furtwangen.
